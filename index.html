Rate My Fart - Support Documentation

Getting Started

Microphone Access

The app requires microphone access to function. When you first launch the app, you'll be prompted to allow microphone access. If you denied access initially, you can enable it in:

  • Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone > Rate My Fart

Basic Usage

  1. Launch the app
  2. Tap the microphone button to start recording
  3. Tap anywhere on the screen to stop recording
  4. View your rating and detailed analysis
  5. Choose to share or record another sound

Features

Sound Analysis

The app measures several audio metrics:

  • Duration (in seconds)
  • Peak sound level (in decibels)
  • Average sound level (in decibels)

Rating System

Our "Sphincter Scale" provides:

  • A score from 0.1 to 10.0
  • Humorous descriptions for each rating
  • Visual representation of the score

Sharing

  • Tap the "Share Recording" button to share your recording
  • Recordings can be shared via any available sharing method on your device
  • Recordings are saved locally until you share or record a new one

Troubleshooting

No Sound Recording

  1. Check microphone permissions
  2. Ensure no other apps are using the microphone
  3. Try closing and reopening the app
  4. Check if your device's microphone is working in other apps

App Crashes

  1. Ensure you're running the latest version
  2. Restart your device
  3. Reinstall the app if problems persist

Poor Sound Quality

  1. Remove any case or covering near the microphone
  2. Ensure proper distance from sound source
  3. Avoid extremely loud sounds that might cause distortion

Privacy

Data Collection

  • All sound processing happens locally on your device
  • No recordings or data are sent to external servers
  • Recordings are temporary and replaced with each new recording

Storage

  • Recordings are stored only temporarily
  • Each new recording replaces the previous one
  • Shared recordings are saved to your device's shared storage

Technical Requirements

  • iOS 16.0 or later
  • Compatible with iPhone and iPad
  • Requires microphone access

Tips for Best Results

  1. Hold device at appropriate distance
  2. Avoid background noise
  3. Wait for the visual sound meter to respond
  4. Record in a quiet environment
  5. Allow the full sound to complete before stopping

Known Issues

  • Background app refresh may stop recording
  • Some older devices may experience delayed visual feedback
  • Share function may take a moment to process large recordings
