  <h1>Rate My Fart - Support Documentation</h1>


  <h2>Getting Started</h2>


  <h3>Microphone Access</h3>

  <p>

    The app requires microphone access to function. When you first launch the app, you'll be prompted to allow microphone access. If you denied access initially, you can enable it in:

  </p>

  <ul>

    <li>Settings &gt; Privacy &amp; Security &gt; Microphone &gt; Rate My Fart</li>

  </ul>


  <h3>Basic Usage</h3>

  <ol>

    <li>Launch the app</li>

    <li>Tap the microphone button to start recording</li>

    <li>Tap anywhere on the screen to stop recording</li>

    <li>View your rating and detailed analysis</li>

    <li>Choose to share or record another sound</li>

  </ol>


  <h2>Features</h2>


  <h3>Sound Analysis</h3>

  <p>The app measures several audio metrics:</p>

  <ul>

    <li>Duration (in seconds)</li>

    <li>Peak sound level (in decibels)</li>

    <li>Average sound level (in decibels)</li>

  </ul>


  <h3>Rating System</h3>

  <p>Our "Sphincter Scale" provides:</p>

  <ul>

    <li>A score from 0.1 to 10.0</li>

    <li>Humorous descriptions for each rating</li>

    <li>Visual representation of the score</li>

  </ul>


  <h3>Sharing</h3>

  <ul>

    <li>Tap the "Share Recording" button to share your recording</li>

    <li>Recordings can be shared via any available sharing method on your device</li>

    <li>Recordings are saved locally until you share or record a new one</li>

  </ul>
